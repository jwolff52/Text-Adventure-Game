It has been a long and hard journey ever since you left home. Slowly moving your way
across Daclovia for the past year and a half, all you have left is 30 gold a small
dagger and patchwork leather that barely passes for clothing. It is just before dawn
and you must leave the barn you stayed in overnight before the farmer catches you.
As you quietly gather yourself you hear a yelp of fear and the slamming of the barn
doors below. Dropping to your knees to stay out of sight you accidentally drop your
dagger and the man yells out in a low, gruff voice. "As if I didn't have enough to
worry about! Who's there!"...
